# PM Skills

## Purpose
This skill supports structured product planning, brand analysis, and proposal development.

## When to Use
- When analyzing a brand's positioning.
- When generating product proposals.
- When synthesizing market and trend insights.

## Instructions
1. Analyze the brand’s positioning, target audience, and key categories.
2. Identify relevant market and consumer trends.
3. Generate proposal-ready outputs including:
   - Stable Options: Immediately manufacturable concepts.
   - Expanded Options: Differentiated concepts requiring further validation.
4. Structure the response in a B2B-ready format suitable for client presentations.
